Introduction to Cells

Cells are the basic structural and functional units of all living organisms. They are often called the "building blocks of life". Every living thing is made up of one or more cells.

There are two major types of cells: prokaryotic and eukaryotic. While both contain essential components like DNA and a cell membrane, eukaryotic cells have membrane-bound organelles, including a nucleus.

This lesson covers:

  • Definition and characteristics of cells
  • Discovery and history of cell biology
  • Cell theory principles
  • Differences between prokaryotes and eukaryotes
